Tree House

Built high among the trees, the Tree House gives a natural and adventurous vibe to the server. It offers a safe and creative hideout above the ground, blending survival style with a peaceful forest atmosphere.

Fish House

A cozy waterside build made for peaceful survival living, the Fish House brings a calm and simple charm to the server. With its fishing theme and relaxed design, it feels like the perfect spot for players to gather, fish, trade stories, or take a break from the chaos of the zombie world.

Townhall

The Townhall stands as the heart of the community, representing order, teamwork, and progress. It serves as a central landmark where players can meet, plan, and feel connected, making it one of the most important builds in the town.

Watch Tower

The Watch Tower is a strong defensive structure built to overlook the surrounding area. It gives players a clear view of incoming danger and adds a serious survival feel, making it both useful and iconic for the server's zombie-themed world.
